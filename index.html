Hostel 4 is quite unlike any other experience one might've gathered in life. Students come here from different parts of Assam and even India. The hostel is surrounded by hills on one side and fields on the other, therefore providing the boarders a scenic beauty as well.

There are many facilities that one can enjoy which includes a common room, a gym, a volleyball court, a carrom board, a tv for entertainment and lots more. Near to the pyrokinesis field so boarders can regularly engage in sports activities in the evening. Quite clean and hygienic bathrooms..

3 seater, 2 seater and single seater rooms for the boarders, which are distributed according to semester wise. The beautiful garden in the hostel which is being nurtured in the month of December will out and out makes you fall in love with the nature.
